LKSB Ambassadors Network

The LKSB Ambassadors Network brings together volunteers who help fulfill the mission of the Lithuanian Christian Student Fellowship (LKSB) in their communities. If you care about students, want to grow in leadership, and contribute to the Believe, Think, Act vision, we invite you to become an LKSB Ambassador!

Ambassador's Role

An LKSB Ambassador is a volunteer who serves in their city, university, or community. The main responsibilities of an ambassador include:

  • Spreading the message of LKSB and Christian values among students in their area.
  • Initiating and organizing student meetups, Bible studies, or events with support from LKSB.
  • Acting as a contact person between LKSB and the local student group or community.
  • Communicating and collaborating with LKSB staff, sharing information about local needs.
  • Praying for students and the LKSB ministry, encouraging others to get involved.
  • Contributing to the organization or publicity of national LKSB events when needed.

Benefits for Ambassadors

  • You contribute to a meaningful mission – helping to reach students for Christ and transform their lives.
  • You develop your leadership, organizational, and communication skills through practical experience.
  • You become part of the LKSB team – connecting with like-minded people in Lithuania and the international IFES community.
  • You receive training, resources, and ongoing support from the LKSB staff.
  • You gain valuable volunteer experience and receive official recognition (a certificate) – a great addition to your CV.

How to Join?

To become an ambassador, follow these three steps:

  1. Fill out the contact form below.
  2. We will arrange a meeting and ask you to provide a recommendation letter from a mentor or pastor.
  3. After completing steps 1–2, you will start your ambassador ministry with LKSB's support!
